Patna Metro

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

Short version: Patna Metro does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No account is required and no information leaves your device.

What We Collect

We collect no personal information. The app does not ask for your name, email, phone number, location, or any other identifying details.

Your language preference (English or Hindi) is saved locally on your device using browser storage. This data never leaves your device.

No Tracking or Analytics

We do not use any analytics tools, advertising SDKs, crash reporters, or third-party trackers. There are no cookies used for tracking purposes.

No Account Required

You do not need to create an account or log in to use any feature of the app. All features — route planning, fare calculation, metro map, and schedules — are fully available without sign-in.

Offline Use

Patna Metro works offline. All timetables, fares, and station data are bundled with the app and do not require an internet connection after the first load.

Third-Party Services

The app is hosted on Vercel. Vercel may collect standard server access logs (IP address, browser type, page visited) as part of normal web hosting operations. These logs are handled according to Vercel's Privacy Policy.

No other third-party services are integrated.

Children's Privacy

This app is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data, there are no special risks for children using the app.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, the new version will be posted at this URL with an updated date. We will not make changes that retroactively affect previously collected data, since we collect none.
